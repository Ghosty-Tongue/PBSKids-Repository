Glossary

Show Title
The title of the show.
Example: "Sesame Street"
Title
The title of the episode or clip.
Example: "Elmo's World: Penguins"
Description
A brief description of the episode or clip.
Example: "Elmo learns about penguins and where they live."
Premiered On
The date when the episode or clip premiered.
Example: "2021-05-12"
Object Type
The type of content (e.g., clip, full_length).
Example: "full_length"
Duration
The length of the episode or clip in seconds, converted to minutes and seconds.
Example: "5:30" (5 minutes and 30 seconds)
Image URL
The URL of the image associated with the episode or clip.
Example: "https://example.com/images/elmo_penguins.jpg"
Video URL
The URL of the video associated with the episode or clip.
Example: "https://example.com/videos/elmo_penguins.mp4"
